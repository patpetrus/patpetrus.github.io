Pat Petrus

A self-starting music entrepreneur with a passion for community development and mentor-based education, Pat Petrus frequently partners with local venues and musicians in efforts to spur the continued economic and artistic development of Indianapolis. As a performing artist, Petrus has organized and participated in performances with the likes of NEA Jazz Master Benny Golson, Grammy award-winning bassist Christian McBride, Grammy award-winning vocalist Kurt Elling, and former director of the United States Marine Band Michael Colburn.
